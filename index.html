SixthSense

A software interface to the real world

SixthSense uses a projector and camera to make amazing things happen.

SixthSense is a wearable gestural interface that augments the physical world around us with digital information and lets us use natural hand gestures to interact with that information.

However, there are several ports in progress to Android, iOS, Linux and more.
